About Me

Hiya, I'm Nikki! I'm a third year Game Design and Development major at the Rochester Institute of Technology. My pronouns are she/they, and I'm super passionate about linux, pixel art, game development, open source software, and all things coding in general!

I'm familiar with and have done work in many game engines such as Unity, Godot, and Unreal. I've also worked with many frameworks and development tools such as MonoGame/XNA, AGS/Astal, p5.js, OpenGL with freeGLUT, and more! I'm even building my own game engine!
